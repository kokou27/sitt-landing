01

Overview

🔒
The short version: SITT processes images locally in your browser. We never sell your data. The only personal information we collect is your email address when you purchase AI credits — handled securely by LemonSqueezy and Supabase.

SITT ("Screen & Image Text Tool") is a Chrome extension developed by Rouff Design. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it when you use our extension and website at sitt-landing.vercel.app.

By installing and using SITT, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the extension.

02

Data we collect

We collect the minimum data required to operate the extension. Here is a complete breakdown:

Data Why Where stored
Email address To associate your AI credits with your purchase (Pro users only) Supabase (encrypted)
Extension ID Anonymous device identifier to manage your credit balance Supabase + Chrome local storage
Credit balance & scan count To track and display your remaining AI credits Supabase + Chrome local storage
OCR history To display your past captures in the sidebar Chrome local storage only (your device)
Settings & preferences OCR language, document mode, translation preferences Chrome sync storage (your Chrome account)
Selected screen area Sent to Gemini Vision API for AI OCR (Pro users only, on demand) Not stored — processed and discarded
We never collect: browsing history, passwords, personal communications, location data, financial information, or any data from pages you visit.
03

OCR processing

SITT offers two OCR modes with different privacy profiles:

📡
Tesseract OCR (Free & Pro Fast mode) — runs 100% locally in your browser using WebAssembly. No image data leaves your device. No internet connection required.
Gemini Vision AI (Pro Quality mode) — when you trigger an AI scan, only the selected screen area is sent to our backend (hosted on Vercel), which forwards it to Google's Gemini Vision API. The image is processed and immediately discarded — it is never stored on our servers or Google's servers beyond the duration of the API call.

Your OCR history (extracted text) is stored only in Chrome's local storage on your own device. It is never uploaded to any server.

04

Third-party services

SITT integrates with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Gemini Vision API — used for AI-powered OCR. Only the selected image area is sent, on demand, when you use Quality mode. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • LemonSqueezy — our payment processor. Handles all payment transactions. We never see or store your credit card details. Governed by LemonSqueezy's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — our database provider. Stores your email and credit balance (Pro users only). Data is encrypted at rest. Governed by Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — hosts our backend API and landing page. Standard access logs may be retained for up to 30 days. Governed by Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Translate / MyMemory / LibreTranslate — used for translation. Only the extracted text is sent when you request a translation. No personal data is included.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for advertising or marketing purposes.

05

Data storage & retention

Data Retention
OCR history Stored locally on your device. Deleted when you clear history or uninstall the extension. Free users: 7 days. Pro users: unlimited.
Settings Stored in Chrome sync storage. Deleted when you uninstall the extension.
Email & credit balance Retained as long as you have an active account. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.
Selected images (AI OCR) Not retained. Processed in real-time and immediately discarded.
Payment records Retained by LemonSqueezy as required by law (typically 7 years for financial records).

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to specific processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the form below. We will respond within 30 days.

For local data (OCR history, settings), you can delete it directly from the extension sidebar at any time without contacting us.

07

Children's privacy

SITT is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
